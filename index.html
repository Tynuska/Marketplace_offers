WHAT IS THE OFFERS INFOTAINMENT APP?
Offers is an in-car app developed by ŠKODA. The infotainment display shows drivers the special bargains available from our contractual partners on their route and, if they are interested, it guides them to the partner’s address. All offers are unique and available only to ŠKODA owners using the Offers app. All drivers need do is present the contractual partner with a voucher sent via the infotainment system to their email address. The app is completely free for ŠKODA customers.
